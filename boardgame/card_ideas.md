# Card Ideas

All cards are written for tableau-style play: Personnel cards stay in the Tableau and refresh each turn; Event cards are single-use and trashed on activation. No hand, no draw pile, no discard pile.

Starting Personnel cards (Builder, Guide, Liaison, Elder) are not listed here — they are defined in rules_v2.md.

---

## Personnel Cards
*For the Talent track. 16 new cards; 20 total including the 4 starting cards.*

When activated, each Personnel card either triggers its primary action **or** provides access to a market — whichever the player chooses. Market access for Talent track Personnel works the same as for starting Personnel (see rules_v2.md).

---

### Architect
*Manages permits and keeps construction costs down.*

When activated: pay 1 fewer Coin when building this turn (minimum 0). Also generates a **Planning Permission** Event card and adds it to your Tableau.

- **Market access:** Politics Market
- **Affinity:** Hotelier, Bureaucrat

---

### Geologist
*Knows what the land is hiding before anyone else does.*

When activated: privately inspect up to 3 Fog hexes — look at their terrain type without revealing them to other players. Place a private marker on each. Additionally, your Industrial Zones adjacent to Mountain hexes generate +1 Ore during your next Procurement.

- **Market access:** Resource Market
- **Affinity:** Industrialist

---

### Harbormaster
*Controls what moves in and out of the island's ports.*

When activated: each of your buildings on a Sand hex adjacent to at least one Water hex generates 1 additional resource during your next Procurement. Also generates a **Port Access** Event card and adds it to your Tableau.

- **Market access:** Resource Market
- **Affinity:** Hotelier, Industrialist

---

### Publicist
*Turns tourism into political leverage.*

When activated: gain 1 Vote per Resort you control. If you have 3 or more Resorts, gain 1 additional Vote.

- **Market access:** Politics Market
- **Affinity:** Hotelier

---

### Engineer
*Solves problems other builders won't touch.*

When activated: build on a Mountain hex (normally forbidden for non-Chieftain players), following all other standard building rules. Alternatively, reduce the Wood and Ore cost of any build action this turn by 1 each.

- **Market access:** Resource Market
- **Affinity:** Industrialist, Bureaucrat

---

### Diplomat
*Deals in favors as much as resources.*

When activated: take 1 resource of any type from another player (they choose which), OR give 2 resources of any type to any player and gain 3 Votes.

- **Market access:** Politics Market
- **Affinity:** Bureaucrat, Chieftain

---

### Cartographer
*Charts the island's interior before anyone else moves in.*

When activated: privately inspect any 4 Fog hexes — look at their terrain type without revealing them. Place private markers only you can see. Generates a **Surveyed Map** Event card and adds it to your Tableau.

- **Market access:** Talent track
- **Affinity:** Guide-heavy strategies

---

### Banker
*Turns liquidity into leverage.*

When activated: gain 1 Coin for every 4 Coins you currently hold (rounded down), representing interest. Alternatively, convert up to 3 Wood or Ore into Coins at the cheapest current market slot price without removing any tokens from the Resource Market.

- **Market access:** Resource Market
- **Affinity:** Hotelier

---

### Developer
*Sees potential in what's already built.*

When activated: upgrade one of your existing buildings to the next tier (Housing → Resort; Farm → Industrial Zone; Civic Office → Infrastructure) paying only 1 Coin — no Wood or Ore required. Also generates a **Renovation** Event card and adds it to your Tableau.

- **Market access:** Talent track
- **Affinity:** Hotelier, Industrialist, Bureaucrat

---

### Tour Operator
*Knows which beaches the tourists actually want.*

When activated: gain 2 Coins for each of your Resorts that is on a Sand hex adjacent to at least one Water hex. Generates a **Package Deal** Event card and adds it to your Tableau.

- **Market access:** Politics Market
- **Affinity:** Hotelier

---

### Naturalist
*Protects the island's ecology — and profits from it.*

When activated: place a Conservation token on any Forest hex for free. Then gain 1 Coin per Conservation token currently on the map (any player's).

- **Market access:** Talent track
- **Affinity:** Chieftain, anyone contesting Forest hexes

---

### Inspector
*Nothing gets built without their sign-off.*

When activated: choose one — look at all of one opponent's current resources (they reveal them to you); OR prevent any single player from placing a building on their next turn.

- **Market access:** Politics Market
- **Affinity:** Bureaucrat

---

### Contractor
*Gets the job done fast when budget isn't an issue.*

When activated: take the Build action twice this turn, each following normal rules and paying full cost. Cannot be combined with any other build discount.

- **Market access:** Resource Market
- **Affinity:** Industrialist, Hotelier, Bureaucrat

---

### Magistrate
*The rules bend toward whoever controls the permit office.*

When activated: remove any 1 card from the Politics market at no cost (it is trashed). Then gain 1 Vote per Infrastructure hex on the map (any player's).

- **Market access:** Politics Market
- **Affinity:** Bureaucrat

---

### Fisherman
*The coastline has always fed the people here.*

When activated: gain 1 Coin per Water hex adjacent to any of your buildings. Generates a **Fishing Rights** Event card and adds it to your Tableau.

- **Market access:** Resource Market
- **Affinity:** Chieftain, Hotelier

---

### Correspondent
*Knows what's coming before it arrives.*

When activated: look at the top 3 cards of the Politics draw deck and rearrange them in any order (do not reveal to other players). Alternatively, copy the effect of the last Event card any player activated this round.

- **Market access:** Politics Market or Talent track (choose one)
- **Affinity:** Bureaucrat, information-heavy strategies

---

## Event Cards

### Politics Market Events
*New additions to the 21 existing Politics market cards.*

---

**Grand Opening**
Gain 3 Coins. If you control 3 or more Resorts, gain 6 Coins instead.
*Best for: Hotelier*

---

**Environmental Impact Study**
Reveal all Fog hexes adjacent to any one non-Fog hex anywhere on the map. Unlike Guide, the target hex does not need to be adjacent to your buildings.
*Best for: anyone mid-game with limited Guide range*

---

**Resettlement**
Move one of your existing buildings to any valid adjacent non-Fog hex. The moved building must still satisfy all adjacency requirements in its new location.
*Best for: anyone whose early placement has become suboptimal*

---

**Strike Action**
Choose a player. On their next turn, they may only place 1 Action Token instead of 2.
*Best for: disrupting a player on the verge of a Mandate purchase*

---

**Tourism Boom**
Gain 1 Coin per Resort on the map (any player's) that is adjacent to at least one Water hex.
*Best for: Hotelier in a game with multiple developed coastlines*

---

**Mineral Rights**
Place a Mineral Rights token on any Mountain or Field hex. You gain 1 Ore each time any player builds on or adjacent to that hex.
*Best for: Industrialist blocking expansion corridors*

---

**Concession**
Choose any non-Fog hex. No player may build on that hex for 2 full rounds.
*Best for: protecting strategic positions or blocking opponents*

---

**Trade Agreement**
Choose a player. For the rest of this round, both you and that player pay 1 fewer Coin when buying from the Resource Market.
*Best for: creating short-term alliances when both players need resources*

---

**Eminent Domain**
Remove any one building from the map. If it is yours, gain resources equal to its build cost. If it belongs to another player, pay them 2 Coins.
*Best for: repositioning or disrupting a well-placed opponent building*

---

**Joint Venture**
You and one other player each immediately place one building adjacently; neither pays the Coin cost. Both buildings must follow all other placement rules. The other player must agree.
*Best for: mutual expansion into contested territory*

---

### Personnel-Generated Event Cards
*Added to a player's Tableau when their Personnel card is activated. Single-use.*

---

**Planning Permission** *(generated by Architect)*
Place one building on any non-Fog hex ignoring the adjacency requirement. All other build rules (cost, hex type, Fog restriction) still apply.

---

**Surveyed Map** *(generated by Cartographer)*
Reveal up to 3 Fog hexes of your choice anywhere on the map. These become public knowledge (unlike Cartographer's private inspection).

---

**Renovation** *(generated by Developer)*
Double the resource production of one of your buildings during your next Procurement. That building generates twice as many Coins, Wood, Ore, or Votes from its adjacencies.

---

**Package Deal** *(generated by Tour Operator)*
Gain 1 Coin per building adjacent to a Water hex — any player's buildings count, not just yours.

---

**Port Access** *(generated by Harbormaster)*
Buy or sell up to 6 Wood or Ore (instead of the usual 4) in a single Resource Market action.

---

**Fishing Rights** *(generated by Fisherman)*
Place a Fishing Rights token on any Water hex. You gain 1 Coin each time another player builds on a hex adjacent to that Water hex.

---

**Geological Survey** *(generated by Geologist)*
Your next Expedition (Guide action) reveals twice as many adjacent Fog hexes — all Fog hexes within 2 tiles of the target are revealed.
