# Landgrab — Player Rulebook

## Goal

Be the first player to earn **4 Seats** by purchasing Mandate cards.

---

## Your Turn

You get **2 Actions** per turn. Each action is one of:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draw** | Draw a card from your draw pile (reshuffles discard if empty) |
| **Play** | Play a Personnel or Event card from your hand |

No hand limit. You can draw 2, play 2, or mix and match.

---

| **Explorer** 🧗‍♀️ | Expedition | |
| **Fixer** 👨🏻‍💼 | Graft | Acquired from Conference |
| **Broker** 🧑‍💼 | Import or Export | Acquired from Conference |
| **Forester** 👷🏿 | Logging or Forestry | Acquired from Conference |
| **Consultant** 🏌️ | Reorganization | Acquired from Conference |

Played from hand, then **trashed** (removed from the game permanently).

## Player Types — Quick Reference

### Hotelier 🏨
- **Builds:** Resort, Housing (Sand/Field hexes)
- **Constraint:** Resorts ≤ Housing count
- **Procurement:** Each Resort earns 1 💰 per adjacent Forest/Water/Mountain. +1 💰 bonus if Resort is on Sand adjacent to Water.
- **Mandate cost:** 10 💰 + Seat#

### Industrialist 🏭
- **Builds:** Industrial Zone, Farm (Sand/Field hexes)
- **Constraint:** Industrial Zones ≤ Farm count. Must build adjacent to Farm/Industrial Zone/Infrastructure.
- **Procurement:** Each Industrial Zone earns 1 🪵 per adjacent Forest, 1 ⚙️ per adjacent Mountain.
- **Mandate cost:** 10 🪵+⚙️ (any mix) + Seat#

### Bureaucrat 🏛️
- **Builds:** Infrastructure, Civic Office (Sand/Field hexes)
- **Constraint:** Infrastructure ≤ Civic Office count. Must build adjacent to Village/Farm/Housing/Industrial Zone/Resort/Civic Office.
- **Procurement:** Each Infrastructure earns 1 🗳️ per adjacent building (each hex counted once).
- **Mandate cost:** 10 🗳️ + Seat#

### Chieftain 🏕️
- **Builds:** Village, Reserve (Reserve on Sand/Field/Forest/Mountain)
- **Constraint:** Reserve must be adjacent to Village or Reserve. Reserve cost = 1 💰 + 1 💰 per existing Reserve.
- **Procurement:** 1 💰 per Reserve not adjacent to any other player's building.
- **Mandate cost:** Presence Score ≥ 10 + Seat# (Presence = Reserves + Villages adjacent to a Reserve)

## Markets

### Resource Market

Buy cheapest available, sell to highest-priced empty slot.

```
Price:   1   |   2   |   3   |   4
-------------------------------------
Ore:         |       |   ●   |   ●      ← starting layout
Wood:        |       |   ●   |   ●
```

- **Buy:** Pay coin price of each slot. Cheapest first. (Buy 1–4 of one type per Procurement.)
- **Sell:** Fill the most expensive empty slot first. Gain those coin values. (Sell 1–4 of one type per Procurement.)
- **End of turn:** If a resource row is completely empty, 1 resource is placed at the 4-Coin slot.

### Politics Market

```
Cost:    1   |   2   |   3   |   4
-------------------------------------
Card:   [ ]  |  [ ]  |  [ ]  |  [ ]
```

- Buy 1 card per Procurement by paying its slot cost.
- Cards shift left after purchase; new card fills the 4-Coin slot from the deck.
- If non-Bureaucrat pays 3+ 💰 for a card, the Bureaucrat gains 1 🗳️.

## Conference (Auction)

Bid on Personnel cards from the Conference row by discarding one of your own.

1. **Select** a Conference card you want.
2. **Discard** a Personnel card from your hand (goes to discard pile regardless of outcome).
3. **Open** with a bid of at least 1 💰.
4. **Clockwise**, each other player gets **1 chance** to:
   - **Pass**, or
   - **Counter-bid**: match the full current bid + add exactly 1 resource (💰, 🪵, ⚙️, or 🗳️).
5. **Winner** pays all bid resources to the Bank (🪵/⚙️ used in bids are destroyed, not returned to market).
6. Winner adds the Conference card to their discard pile.

**Bids accumulate:** 3 💰 → 3 💰 + 1 🪵 → 3 💰 + 1 🪵 + 1 🗳️ ...

Choosing a rare resource type can **lock out** opponents who don't have it.

Empty Conference slots are refilled at the end of each round.

---

## Winning: Mandate → Promotion → Seat

| Step | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| **Mandate** | Must be your **first action**. Pay your type's cost (see table below). Adds Promotion + Seat to discard. **Ends your turn.** |
| **Promotion** | Auto-plays when drawn. Discard your hand. Adds Dividends to discard. |
| **Seat** | Play to gain 1 Seat. **4 Seats wins the game.** |

### Mandate Costs

| Player | Cost | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Hotelier | 10 💰 + Seat# | |
| Industrialist | 10 🪵+⚙️ + Seat# | Any mix |
| Bureaucrat | 10 🗳️ + Seat# | |
| Chieftain | Presence ≥ 10 + Seat# | Presence = Reserves + Villages adj. to Reserve |

Mandate always occupies the **4-Coin slot** in the Politics market. It cannot be Bribed or removed — only purchased.

## Building Placement Rules

| Building | Hex Types | Adjacency Required | Player |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| Resort | Sand, Field | Resort, Housing, or Infrastructure | Hotelier |
| Housing | Sand, Field | Resort, Housing, or Infrastructure | Hotelier |
| Industrial Zone | Sand, Field | Farm, Industrial Zone, or Infrastructure | Industrialist |
| Farm | Sand, Field | Farm, Industrial Zone, or Infrastructure | Industrialist |
| Infrastructure | Sand, Field | Village, Farm, Housing, Ind. Zone, Resort, or Civic Office | Bureaucrat |
| Civic Office | Sand, Field | Village, Farm, Housing, Ind. Zone, Resort, or Civic Office | Bureaucrat |
| Village | Any non-Water | (via Charter or Contact only) | Chieftain |
| Reserve | Sand, Field, Forest, Mountain | Village or Reserve | Chieftain |

- **Build** costs 1 🪵 + 1 ⚙️ + 1 💰 (except Reserve, which costs 💰 only).
- **Charter** ignores adjacency and cost requirements.
- No building may be placed adjacent to Fog (except via Charter/Contact).
